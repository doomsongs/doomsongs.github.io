The first #browniehome influencers triggered the usual suspects. Only the finest organic dairy for your Brownie, click to order! Interrogating the ethics of Brownie Spotter Cams. Shouldn't Brownies pay taxes like everyone else? Ten things to say instead of "Thank you." Wait, I can say "Brownie," but I can't say-

A few months later, I stumbled into my kitchen to find it spotless. Countertop gleaming, sink empty--even the magnetic poetry scattered on my fridge since 2003 now read like something you'd see, uncredited, on Instagram. I tried to cram this phenomenon into 1080 by 608, experimenting with angles and zoom, but I couldn't capture what made my apartment so extraordinary that morning. The TV, dust-free in the living room just beyond the linoleum, remained cheap and unimpressive. The withered artifacts from a bad date atop it, however, were now tastefully arranged and believable as intentional décor rather than negligence.

Stuffing my phone into my bathrobe pocket, I rummaged through the fridge and sniffed the carton of half-and-half I found in the back. Passable. When I placed the offering by the back door, I expected my cat to expropriate it immediately, entitled as she believed herself to be, but she barely looked up. A new normal had been established, and nobody needed to say anything.
